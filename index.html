What is $MEMES?

$MEMES is a community token launching on pump.fun (Solana) in July 2026. Trading fees fund a treasury that buys other pump.fun tokens and distributes them to $MEMES holders every week.

How do the holder rewards work?

The pump.fun creator fee is split 50/50. Half funds a treasury that buys other pump.fun tokens and redistributes them to eligible holders weekly. Half funds development. The mechanism is active from the very first trade.

When does $MEMES launch?

$MEMES launches in July 2026 on pump.fun. The official contract address is posted on x.com/memecoinsoul (pinned) and on memecoinssol.xyz at the same time, and nowhere else.

Is there a presale or team allocation?

No. $MEMES is a fair launch with 0% team allocation and no presale. 100% goes to the community.

How do I qualify for the weekly rewards?

Hold at least $10 of $MEMES continuously through the weekly snapshot window. Full rules are in the whitepaper.

What is the Fun IRL Hub?
